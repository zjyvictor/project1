 Judd Trump: The Aces

Judd Trump (born 20 August 1989)[8] is an English professional snooker player from Bristol who is the reigning World Champion and the current world number one. One of 11 players in the history of the sport to win all Triple Crown events at least once, he has won a career total of 17 ranking titles, putting him in joint seventh place (with Mark Selby) on the all-time list.
